About Me



I am your average citizen who has found that borders do not limit selection of goods and services as a consumer. My wallet speaks for myself and if I can save a dollar, then why not? I've seen other people online complaining about the exact situation that I've found myself in so that's why I have extended my services to you.

Item Selection
The USA has much better product selection and blows Canada away. You'll often find items that are only available for US markets and would cost alot of money to import to Canada, and in some cases retailers won't even ship to Canada! Don't let borders bind your item selection. Let me take care of the border hopping for you.

Savings
In addition to having better selection, items are significantly cheaper in the US compared to Canada. This is the main consideration of cross border shopping. Even after taxes and conversion of the CAD, you still end up saving as little as 20% and sometimes even more by shopping in the US.

Let me save you money!

Services

My service offerings are simple. You will contact me and inform me of the item you wish to purchase. I will respond to you with the final value of the cost of the item (including shipping, taxes, conversion and service fee). If you wish to purchase the item, you will pay me the cost of the final cost of the item and I will then purchase it for you.

I only purchase from eBay and Amazon from reputable sellers. In some cases make exceptions to this rule and may purchase from big box retailers but that is it. This is to ensure that my services are not missused for other purposes and for my own well-being and safety. I do not traffick any goods and report the full value of all items.

Philosophy

My philosophy is to save you as much money as I can. I like to identify as a bargain hunter and sometimes it spills over to my desire to help others as well. I am a believer of value for your money and this is what separates me from package forwarders and personal shoppers. I can save you even more money than using their services and expensive package shipping costs from the US to Canada that you end up paying as the consumer. I make frequest trips to the US and import my own items, why not extend the offer to others. For a small fee for my services, I will can save you the most money when purchasing items.
